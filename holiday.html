National Pfeffernusse Day | December 23

Pfeffernusse Day falls on December 23 every year. This festive cookie is a family favourite for Germans, Dutch, Danish, and many other European families. This holiday being so close to Christmas makes it the perfect day to prepare these to gift guests on Christmas Day.
