18 Intriguing Facts About Jonathan Banks

#Fact: Jonathan Banks was paid $100,000 for each episode he played Michael Ehrmantraut in the Breaking Bad spinoff Better Call Saul!

Actor Jonathan Banks is most famous for his role in the television series Breaking Bad which was followed by the spin-off Better Call Saul.

His career took off in the 1980s when he featured in some top movies and his success hasn’t stopped since.

He is known for playing the bad guy or villain in some of the world’s most loved television series and films.

Here are 18 facts about Jonathan Banks that you should know.

  1. Jonathan Ray Banks is 75 years old. He was born on January 31, 1947, in Washington, D.C.

  2. He was raised by his mother Elena Adams Banks in Chillum Heights, Maryland. Elena worked as a secretary across government offices including the CIA.

  3. Banks graduated in 1966 from Northwood High School in Silver Spring.

  4. He attended Indiana University Bloomington where he studied alongside actor Kevin Kline. They participated in The Threepenny Opera together.

  5. Banks met his first wife to be, Marnie Fausch at university and they married in 1968. Sadly they divorced in 1970.

  6. Banks was given the opportunity to work as stage manager for the touring company “Hair”, so he dropped out of university to pursue this opportunity. Part of the tour took them to Australia.

  7. The 1980s were when Banks’ career took off with his first three big roles in the films Airplane! in 1980, 48 Hrs in 1982, and Beverly Hills Cops in 1984.

  8. His break into television was in 1987 when he played Frank McPike in the series Wiseguy for four years. This role led to an Emmy nomination.

  9. Jonathan Banks re-married in 1990 to Gennera Banks.

  10. Banks has three children. Claudio Jon Henry Banks from his first marriage and Rebecca Elena Banks and Joanna Rae Banks Morgan from his second marriage.

  11. On May 14, 1994, Banks took part in a two-hour-long special episode of Walker, Texas Ranger. The special was called “The Reunion” and featured other famous actors such as Chuck Norris, Clarence Gilyard, and Ben Masters.

  12. From 2004 to 2006, Banks featured in two episodes of CSI: Crime Scene Investigation as Bobby Jensen.

  13. Banks played a role in the 2007 TV series Dexter as the FBI Deputy Director. He appeared in two episodes of Season 2, “Left Turn Head” and “The British Invasion.”

  14. In 2013, Banks won the Best Supporting Actor on Television award at the Saturn Awards for his role in the series Breaking Bad.

  15. In 2015, Banks voiced the character of James Gordon in the video game “Batman: Arkham Knight.”

  16. His daughter Rebecca Elena Banks has followed a similar path to her father in film and TV. She works as a film director and writer.

  17. Rebecca also worked with her father Jonathan Banks on the award-winning AMC series Better Call Saul in 2015.

  18. Banks won his second award in 2015 at the Critics’ Choice Television Awards. The award was for Best Supporting Actor in a Drama Series for his role in Better Call Saul.

#Fact: Jonathan Banks has influenced the television and film industry for decades, playing important support roles and featuring as the bad guy.

He made his career in the 80s playing roles in some of the world’s most loved movies and he hasn’t stopped since.

In more recent years he has taken over television screens with his features in Breaking Bad and Better Call Saul.
