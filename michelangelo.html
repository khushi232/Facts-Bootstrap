10 Magnificent Facts About Michelangelo

#Fact: Did you know that Michelangelo designed the fortifications for his hometown, the city of Florence?

Michelangelo is one of the world’s most famous sculptors and painters of all time.

The Italian renaissance artist influenced the future of Western art and was one of the most successful living artists of his time.

Michelangelo’s works can be seen across Italy, but the Sistine Chapel in the Vatican City is the most known.

Here are 10 magnificent facts about Michelangelo.

  1. As well as being a sculptor and painter, Michelangelo was a poet too.

  2. Michelangelo’s commission of the Sistine chapel was part of a sabotage plot.

  3. He designed the fortifications for the city of Florence.

  4. Michelangelo worked with nine different Popes.

  5. Michelangelo included himself in the Sistine Chapel painting.

  6. Michelangelo was never married.

  7. He was the first living Western artist to publish a biography.

  8. Michelangelo lived like a poor man

  9. He worked up until the week he died.

  10. The Statue of David was carved from a discarded piece of stone.

#Fact: Michelangelo was one of the world’s most influential artists of all time.

His work is admired by thousands of tourists every year, and he is an important part of global art history.

Michelangelo was a man of many talents, and we will continue to learn from his painting and sculpting techniques.
