15 Terrific Facts About Tom Holland That You Didn’t Know

#Fact: Did you know that Tom Holland's first tattoo was a Spider-Man logo on the bottom of his foot?

Tom Holland quickly grew from a struggling young actor to a household name.

He doesn’t seem to be going anywhere soon with a blossoming career!

For more info about the thriving British actor, we have the best 45 facts below.

  1. Tom’s full name is Thomas Stanley Holland. He is 26 years old and was born on June 1, 1996, in Kingston-upon-Thames, England, UK.

  2. Tom Holland has three younger brothers: Harry, Sam, and Paddy.

  3. His father, Dominic Holland, is a stand-up comedian, and his mother, Nicola, is a photographer.

  4. Tom Holland was initially a dancer. He took hip-hop dance classes at Nifty Feet Dance School.

  5. Tom Holland was bullied at Wimbledon College throughout his school years for being a dancer.

  6. Tom Holland was diagnosed with dyslexia when he was a kid.

  7. After Wimbledon, Tom attended BRIT School for Performing Arts.

  8. While performing with his dance group at the Richmond Dance Festival, Holland was discovered by choreographer Lynne Page.

  9. After eight auditions and two years of training, Holland was cast in Billy Elliot the Musical as Michael Caffrey, Billy’s best friend.

  10. His role in Billy Elliot marked his first performance on the West End.

  11. Tom Holland made his first feature film debut in The Impossible in 2012, alongside Naomi Watts and Ewan Mcgregor.

  12. Holland co-starred with Chris Hemsworth in In the Heart of the Sea in 2015.

  13. To prepare for In the Heart of the Sea, Holland had to cut down a lot of weight, eating only 500-1000 calories per day.

  14. After In the Heart of the Sea, Holland’s career had a lull, and his mom had him attend carpentry school.
