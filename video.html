20 Interesting Facts About Your Favorite Video Games

#Fact: Left 4 Dead 2 was so popular that an expansion was released eleven years after the original launch date.

Do you often find yourself reflecting on all the different games you’ve played over the years? If you try and think off the top of your head about the best video games, only a few will come to mind. If you stop and remember all the great games you feel nostalgic about – old or new – you’ll find there’s a lot more than you first thought! We’ve made the monumental effort of reducing that almighty list down to a short, sweet 100, and we’re here to tell you all about them! So stay awhile, and check out these 100 facts about your best gaming moments!

  1. A custom-made demo of SimCity 2000 is exhibited in the New York Museum of Modern Art.

  2. The dragon Paarthurnax in The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim is voiced by the same man who voices Nintendo’s Mario.

  3. Gears of War 2 was featured in AMC’s The Walking Dead.

  4. Left 4 Dead 2 was so popular that an expansion was released 11 years after the original launch date.

  5. Rockstar Games hired real-life gang members to voice background characters in Grand Theft Auto V.

  6. Half-Life was listed as the Best-Selling First-Person Shooter of All Time (PC) in the 2008 gamer’s edition of the Guinness Book of World Records.

  7. The creators of PUBG tried to sue the makers of Fortnite.

  8. There are multiple references in Batman: Arkham City to ABC’s hit TV series Lost.

  9. The studio behind Thief II: The Metal Age ran out of money, so they donated their tools to the game’s fans to expand upon the game.

  10. The initial designs for the Little Sisters in BioShock were absolutely horrifying.

  11. The background noises in Silent Hill 2 were randomized to keep players on the edge of their seats.

  12. God of War (2018) plays out like a movie that’s been shot in one single take, with no cuts or loading screens.

  13. Counter-Strike was initially a custom mod you could play in Half-Life.

  14. Divinity: Original Sin 2 wasn’t meant to be released with voiced dialogue.

  15. In 2012 the developers of Terraria tried to cancel further updates to the game.

  16. X-COM: UFO Defense was nearly canceled twice!

  17. Baldur’s Gate was developed by Bioware, the same developer who would later make the Mass Effect series.

  18. Released in 1991, Neverwinter Nights was the first successful MMORPG.

  19. The creators of Rock Band were the same people who made the first two Guitar Hero games.

  20. In the first 24 hours of its release, more than a million people logged into Xbox Live to play Halo 3.
